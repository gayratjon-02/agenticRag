Acme Corp — Company Handbook (sample document)

About
Acme Corp was founded in 1987 by Jane Doe. Its headquarters are located in
Tashkent, Uzbekistan. The company employs roughly 450 people across three offices.

Products
The flagship product is the WidgetPro 3000, a device used for industrial automation.
A smaller model, the WidgetPro Mini, is aimed at small workshops. Both products come
with a two-year warranty.

Support
Customer support is available Monday to Friday, 9am to 6pm local time. The standard
response time for support tickets is one business day. Premium customers receive
priority support with a four-hour response target.

Returns
Customers may return any product within 30 days of purchase for a full refund,
provided the product is undamaged and in its original packaging.
